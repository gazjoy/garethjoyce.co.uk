About me

My name is Gaz Joyce from Stafford, I am one of a handful of interaction designers working for Valtech in the UK. I help organisations make digital experiences that have real meaning and work anywhere they are accessed.

I have been designing and building web interfaces since I graduated in 2004 with a degree in Multimedia Design. I enjoy simplifying complex design problems by listening to opinion and questioning reasoning.

This is what I am currently focussing on →

Previously, I worked at iWeb in Stafford where I helped design and build a plethora of Magento and WordPress managed brochure and e-commerce products.

Throughout my career I have worked on every detail of creating a successful digital and print design.

  • Graphics
  • Printed products
  • Wireframing
  • Paper prototyping
  • Digital prototyping
  • Interface design
  • Interface development

I care deeply about understanding user needs and transforming them into accessible products with lovely, rich typeset content, that hits the mark on performance. Its what makes me tick.
