Working with the Department for Transport to improve the experience for Blue Badge applicants and process optimisation for local authorities.

To award a Blue Badge, local authorities need to validate that someone applying for a Blue Badge with a DWP benefit is entitled to that benefit.

Our team at Valtech worked with over 50 local authorities from England, Wales and Scotland to understand the disparate ways they were working to validate the benefit entitlement.

We found many were still reliant on the applicant providing a photo or scan of their benefit award letter.

This meant applicants were spending a lot of time digitising physical letters. We also found that some local autohiries had direct access to DWP systems.

This enabled them to validate the benefit entitlement directly.

We were able to collate evidence,

Used task timings, saving applicants over 4 minutes on average.

One of the largest areas of negative user feedback on the digital service is the need for applicants to provide evidence of a qualifying benefit.

We looked at challenging the need for this evidence.
