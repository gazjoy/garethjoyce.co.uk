{{ title }}

  1. Design for everyone: I aim to make everything I do understandable and usable for all
  2. Do just enough: I like to keep things simple and do not believe in putting work out into the world without it having a purpose
  3. Meet the need: I'm user centrered and use research and data to design based on real needs
